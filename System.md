II. Major Suit Openings (1♥ / 1♠)
Opening Standards

    1♥: 5+ Hearts, 10+ HCP (Rule of 20). 1NT rebid requires 12+ HCP.

    1♠: 5+ Spades (4 allowed in 3rd/4th or if GF). 1NT rebid requires 12+ HCP.

Responses

    1NT: Semi-Forcing (Includes 3-card sub-minimum raises, 6 HCP).

    2♣/2♦ (2/1): Game Force (2♣ promises 2+ cards).

    1♠ - 2♥: 5+ Hearts, Game Force.

Raises

    2M: Constructive (Minimum opener, usually 4-card support, rarely 3).

    3M: Mixed Raise (4 trumps, 7–9 HCP).

    2NT: Jacoby 2NT (Forcing Raise, Limit+).

        3♣: Minimum, any shape.

        3♦: Extra values, unspecified shortness (Ask).

        3♥: Extra values, No Shortness.

        3♠: Shortness in Clubs (Low).

        3NT: Shortness in Diamonds (Mid).

        4♣: Shortness in Other Major (High).

        3M: 18+, No shortness, Slam interest.

    Splinters:

        Over 1♥: 3♠, 4♣, 4♦. (3♠ = "Mini-splinter", 7ish losers, min GF).

        Over 1♠: 4♣, 4♦, 4♥ .

    3NT: 13–15 balanced, good 4-card support (or 3 distinct honors).

III. Minor Suit Openings (1♣ / 1♦)
Structure

    1♣: 3+ Clubs (2 if 4=4=3=2).

    1♦: 4+ Diamonds. Denies 4M unless 5+♦ and Inv+.

    Inverted Minors:

        1m - 2m: Limit Raise+, 5+ cards (Force to 3m).

        1m - 3m: Weak/Preemptive.

        2(m+1): Artificial Game Force.

            1♣ - 2♦: Artificial GF.

            1♦ - 2♥: Artificial GF.

Responses to 1m

    1M: 4+ Cards.

    1NT: 6–10 HCP, Balanced.

    2NT: 10–12 HCP, Balanced, no 4M.

    Jump Shifts (2/3-level):

        UPH: 2-level jumps are Weak; 3-level jumps are Invitational.

        BPH/Comp: Fit-showing jumps.

IV. Checkback & Third Suit Forcing
After 1X - 1Y - 1NT

    2♣: Puppet to 2♦ (Forces 2♦).

        Used for weak hands (pass 2♦ or bid 2M) or some invitational hands.

    2♦: Artificial Game Force.

        Opener shows 3-card support for responder's suit first.

    Jumps:

        Jump to 3-level in lower-ranking unbid suit: 5–5 shape.

        Jump to 3-level in higher-ranking unbid suit: Self-Splinter.

Third Suit Forcing (New Oct '25)

    1♣ - 1M; 2♣ - 2♦: Artificial Game Force.

    1♦ - 1♠; 2♦ - 2♥: Artificial Game Force.

VI. Slam Bidding
Keycard (1430)

    1 or 4 Keycards: Bid 3♣/4♣ (depending on level).

    0 or 3 Keycards: Bid 3♦/4♦.

    2 Keycards without Queen: Step 3.

    2 Keycards with Queen: Step 4.

    Void Responses:

        Even Keycards (2 or 4) + Void: Bid 5NT.

        Odd Keycards (1 or 3) + Void: Jump in void suit (or 6T if void is higher).

    Interference over RKC:

        DOPI (Double = 0/3, Pass = 1/4) below 5-trump.

        DEPO (Double = Even, Pass = Odd) above 5-trump.

Non-Serious 3NT

    Applies when Major agreed at 3-level in a GF auction.

    3NT: "I have serious slam interest" (Wait, usually Non-Serious is the artificial bid to deny interest).

        Correction per source: "3S is non serious here... because it fits".

        Clarification: Source 376 says "NS3N bid is 3S" (in hearts).

        Rule: Bidding the "Non-Serious" step denies serious slam interest. Bypassing it shows serious interest.

        Context: 1♥ - 2NT; 3♥... 3♠ is Non-Serious.
